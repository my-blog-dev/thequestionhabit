Introduction

Welcome to The Question Habit — a place where curiosity meets reflection. This is more than a blog. It's a personal challenge to ask one meaningful question every day and explore the answers through research, introspection, or conversation. Here, questions lead the way to deeper understanding, and daily wonder becomes a habit.

Motivation

Have you ever had a random thought that made you pause — like why humans dream or how fonts affect mood? That spark of curiosity is powerful. The Question Habit is built on the belief that asking better questions helps us think better, live more mindfully, and stay forever learning. Join me in exploring the questions we too often ignore.
